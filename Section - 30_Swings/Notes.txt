
difference between AWT vs Swings

AWT :                           Swings : 

Plateform Dependent             Plateforme Independent
Heavy Weight                    Light Weight
Few Components                  Many Components
Host Look&Feel                  Pluggable Look And Feel
No MVC                          Supports MVC
